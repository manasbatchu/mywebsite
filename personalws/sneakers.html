Sneakers

My passion for sneakers started around the same time I grew an obsession in basketball. Being a LeBron fan, I wanted his latest signature sneaker at that time, the LeBron 11. But the shoes were very expensive, so I never got them. It started with basketball shoes, but later, I started to get into the realm of hype sneakers and all that. This year, I finally got a pair of LeBron's. It's the one that you see in the picture. Sadly, I haven't gotten time to wear them because of the quarantine. If I could have any shoe in the world, I would want the Air Jordan 1 Dior. When I grow up, I wish to design sneakers at Nike. Hopefully I design signature basketball sneakers for a big name star like Giannis Antetokounmpo, Luka Doncic, or Jayson Tatum.
